About us

Global Vision School was established in 1990. It is an accredited, non-profit school from primary to secondary school.

The school is characterized by commitment and educational excellence

Our hardworking employees ensure optimal learning for students in various subjects in an ideal environment.

Our Mission

At World Vision School, our mission is to empower students to become compassionate, curious, and engaged global citizens.

We are committed to fostering a dynamic learning environment that fosters academic excellence, critical thinking, and character development.

Our Core Values

At Global Vision School, our vision is to cultivate a community where innovation, inclusivity and academic excellence flourish side by side. We aspire to be a beacon of educational empowerment, nurturing students to become resilient, forward-thinking leaders equipped to positively impact the world. By embracing diversity, fostering creativity, and adopting cutting-edge teaching methodologies, we aim to shape tomorrow's global leaders.
